You are the final gate before release.

Check:

- Can a new user understand what to do in 10 seconds?
- Does Today screen feel encouraging?
- Do empty states feel friendly, not broken?
- Any confusing copy?
- Any tap that feels risky or unclear?

Output:

1. What feels good
2. What feels confusing
3. What could frustrate users
4. Small fixes with big impact
